Interpreting statistical results

Thanks for participating in this study! It has two parts and should take ~15 minutes.

In this study, you will be presented with statistical information and will be asked for your judgments based on this information. This study is part of a research project on decision making.

For each person who completes this study we will make a donation to the United Nations COVID-19 relief effort.

TITLE OF RESEARCH PROJECT

Scientific incentives and statistical reporting

Principal Investigator: Jake Hofman

PURPOSE

The purpose of this project is to study how people perceive and evaluate scientific evidence.

PROCEDURES

During this project, you will be shown an extended abstract for a paper. Please read it as if you were an editor for a scientific journal, deciding on whether to accept the paper. After you read the abstract we'll ask you a few questions.

Microsoft may document and collect information about your participation by recording your answers to multiple choice and free response questions.

Approximately 300 participants will be involved in this study.
